    市场

    交易所市场

    交易品种均为标准化产品。\

    场外市场

    Over-the-counter market(OTC)

    期货及远期

    Forward(远期)

    属于非标准化产品，只在场外市场交易。

    Future(期货)

    属于标准化产品，在交易所市场交易。\
    期货合约的规格：

    • Underlying asset, often graded by quality.

    • Size of each contract. Some exchange provides mini-product for small individual investors.

    • Delivery Day and location

    • minimum trading unit. Crude oil is in cents while long term T-bonds is in 1/32 dollar.

    • limit of movement and maximum position for speculators.

    期货的价格应随着到期日的临近收敛到即期价格。

    Margin System

    In future market, daily settlement or marking to market is applied. So at the end of the day, your profit or loss in the day would reflect in your margin account.\
    To enter the market, investor needs to deposit initial margin in his margin account. When the margin account is lower than the maintenance margin, investor needs to deposit variation margin until the margin account is equal to initial margin, else his position would be closed out. Investor is allowed to withdraw margin that is more than the initial margin.\
    Fundamental investor usually were charged with less margin.

    Clearing-house

    A clearing-house have a limit number of members and only member can trade in the clearing-house. Broker that is not a member must conduct trade through a member. Initial margin or clearing margin is required for each member and the amount is calculated by the net position of the member. Also there is no maintenance margin, so any loss or gain will need to be directly paid or withdraw.

    本质上只需要考虑折价即可。

    Fixed Income

    Interest Rate

    Some common reference on the interest rates:

    • Government Bond: The advantage of government bond yield is that it has many different maturity and can matches all currency. And is almost always the risk free rate. But the rate is commonly lower than the true risk free rate, since return on government bond is tax-free which gives it a premium. Also many large institutions are forced to hold certain amount of government bond which increase its market price and decrease the yield rate.

    • LIBOR: London Interbank Offered Rate is the non-mortgage short term interbank lending rate in London. Normally all banks that gives the pricing is at least AA on credit level. In China, there is the SHIBOR.

    • Federal Funds Rate: It is the interbank lending rate in US that happens in the FED account used for required reserve. FED often control this rate to change the interest rate in the market. Similarly in Britain, there is SONIA(Stering Overnight Index Average) and in Europe there is EONIA(euro overnight index average).

    • Repo Rate: Repo rate is the interest rate on lending that have securities(often government bond) as mortgage. It is often lower than Federal Funds Rate since it involves in mortgage.

    • Risk Free Rate: Traditionally, investors would use LIBOR as risk free rate, but since 2008 people don’t assume AA credit is safe enough so there is growing debate on what kind of rate to reference on.

    Swap(互换)

    期权

    Basics

    Call option is the right to buy at a given time. A put option is the right to sell at a given time.

    • An European option can be exercised only at the end of its life.

    • An American option can be exercised at any time.\
      The best time to exercise an American option is at the end of its maturity. If the investor want to exercise the option, they can make a better profit by selling the option on the market instead of exercise it.

    • Option class is option with the similar underlying asset and position.

    • Option series is option in the same option class and have the same strike price and maturity.
